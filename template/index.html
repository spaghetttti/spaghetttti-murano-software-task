Business Auto Bind Requirements

Documentation is due within five (5) days of binding or a cancellation warning will be sent. Your policy will not be released until all items have been received.

  • Signed and dated ACORDS 125, 127 & 137
  • Signed GMI Supplemental Application (not required for renewals)
  • Insured's FEIN
  • Complete description of operation
  • Complete drivers list
  • Signed Driver Acknowledgement Letter
  • Signed Finance Agreement (if applicable)
  • Signed, dated, and initialed UM Forms
  • Vehicle Registration Copies (for NY accounts only)

Please do not complete forms with coverages not quoted. If you require additional or different coverages, please contact your Underwriter.

All bind requests should be sent directly to your Underwriter and Business Development Representative.
